I am interested in using mathematical techniques and scientific principles to understand various physical phenomena. I currently use continuum mechanics and non-equilibrium thermodynamics to understand lipid membranes.

Theory

Simulation

I apply the framework of irreversible thermodynamics to determine the equations of motion governing lipid membrane behavior.

I employ finite element methods to simulate non-axisymmetric membrane behavior, including viscous in-plane flow and elastic out-of-plane bending.
