About

I work with Prof. Kranthi Mandadapu at UC Berkeley to study the dynamics of lipid bilayers in the cell, using continuum models. We developed a general theory to model such membranes, and I am currently solving the resultant equations of motion.

Before Berkeley, I majored in chemical engineering at Princeton University, where I worked with Prof. Howard Stone.
