About

I work with Prof. Kranthi Mandadapu at UC Berkeley to study the dynamics of lipid bilayers in the cell. I used continuum mechnaics and irreversible thermodynamics to find the equations of motion of arbitrarily curved lipid membranes, and I am now developing finite element software to solve these equations.

Before Berkeley, I majored in chemical engineering at Princeton University, where I worked with Prof. Howard Stone.
